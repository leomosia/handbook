Use escalation when a handbook change is blocked and the missing information is
preventing work.

## Escalation Path

1. Ask on the pull request.
2. Mention the relevant `CODEOWNERS`.
3. If the change affects platform structure, ask the platform owner.
4. If the change affects public positioning, ask the responsible business owner.

Escalation should resolve ownership and accuracy, not bypass review.
