The handbook is improved through pull requests.

Contributors should make focused changes, explain the reason for the change,
and request review from the appropriate owners.

## Good Contributions

- Clarify an existing process.
- Add missing context.
- Move content to its proper source of truth.
- Remove outdated or duplicated material.
- Link related handbook pages together.

## Review

The reviewer is responsible for checking whether the change is clear, belongs in
the selected location, and does not duplicate another source of truth.
